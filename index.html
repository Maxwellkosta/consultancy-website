Introduction

Giowide Solutions is an Accounting, Management, and Consulting firm registered in Kenya. The firm offers Professional management, Accounting, Business Advisory Services, and other related but important business services that inspire growth and sustainability in organizations.

Our client base extends from private enterprises, not-for-profit organizations, and other corporates to government directorates and social enterprises.
